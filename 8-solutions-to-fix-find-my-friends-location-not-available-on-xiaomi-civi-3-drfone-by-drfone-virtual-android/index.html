Part 1: What Does “Location Not Available” Mean on Find My Friends?

When the “Location Not Available” message appears on Find My Friends, it means that you have disabled location sharing or have turned off your device’s location services. Does “Location Not Available” mean that there is something wrong with Xiaomi Civi 3 ?

How to distinguish “No Location Found” and “Location Not Available”?

“No Location Found” means that the Find My app is unable to locate the Xiaomi Civi 3 device being tracked. This can happen for a variety of reasons, such as if the Xiaomi Civi 3 device is turned off, not connected to the internet, or if location services are disabled.

“Location Not Available” means that the Xiaomi Civi 3 device is not currently sharing its location with the Find My app. This can happen if the Xiaomi Civi 3 device is in Airplane mode or if the user has turned off location sharing for the Find My app. It can also happen if there is a temporary issue with the iCloud servers.

Geo-blocking is the practice of restricting online access to certain users depending on where in the world they happen to be physically located. Telecommunications firms, websites, and other content providers and intellectual property owners often use this method for copyright protection. To monitor and enforce geo-blocks, databases that map out IP addresses’ geographical locations are often employed.
