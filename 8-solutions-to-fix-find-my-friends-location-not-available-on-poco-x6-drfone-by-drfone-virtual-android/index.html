Part 1: What Does “Location Not Available” Mean on Find My Friends?

When the “Location Not Available” message appears on Find My Friends, it means that you have disabled location sharing or have turned off your device’s location services. Does “Location Not Available” mean that there is something wrong with Poco X6 ?

How to distinguish “No Location Found” and “Location Not Available”?

“No Location Found” means that the Find My app is unable to locate the Poco X6 device being tracked. This can happen for a variety of reasons, such as if the Poco X6 device is turned off, not connected to the internet, or if location services are disabled.

“Location Not Available” means that the Poco X6 device is not currently sharing its location with the Find My app. This can happen if the Poco X6 device is in Airplane mode or if the user has turned off location sharing for the Find My app. It can also happen if there is a temporary issue with the iCloud servers.
